# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project is an inclusive community. We pledge to make
participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless
of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, caste, colour, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expectations

- Be kind, specific, and honest.
- Assume good faith. Default to curiosity before criticism.
- Focus on the code, the design, the idea — not the person.
- Acknowledge others' contributions.
- Accept constructive feedback without escalation; give it without
  condescension.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or
  political attacks.
- Public or private harassment, trolling, or deliberate
  intimidation.
- Sexualised language or imagery, and unwanted sexual attention.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit
  permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Reports go to the project maintainer (utsav.itsm@gmail.com). All
reports are reviewed and responded to, and confidentiality is
honoured. Consequences scale with severity: warning → temporary ban
→ permanent ban.

## Scope

This Code applies within project spaces (repository issues, pull
requests, discussions, related chat rooms) and when an individual
is representing the project in public.
